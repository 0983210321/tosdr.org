About ToS;DR A transparent and collaborative project to rate terms of services

The ToS;DR project aims at stopping the biggest lie on the web.

Terms of Service; Didn't Read is a project by Unhosted, inspired by Aza Raskin's Privacy Icons and EFF's TOSBack.
